---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributors on how to participate effectively in a project by following consistent, reviewable, and maintainable workflows. This topic covers contributor expectations, conventions, and recommended steps for preparing changes, updating documentation, aligning with existing structure, and communicating progress. It helps onboard new contributors while supporting experienced maintainers with a repeatable process that reduces back-and-forth, improves consistency, preserves quality, and makes collaborative work easier to sustain over time.

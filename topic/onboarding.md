---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers the process of helping new users or contributors get started quickly and confidently. It focuses on clear introductory guidance that explains the core value or concept in simple terms, outlines what to read or do first, and provides a predictable path through setup and early usage. It includes concise onboarding documentation and first-run experiences that teach the workflow through simple, progressive steps, such as checklists or structured first steps. The goal is to reduce confusion and friction, shape positive early impressions, set expectations for how to engage, and help beginners become productive sooner and with less drop-off.

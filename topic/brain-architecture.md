---
title: Brain Architecture
---
The structural design of a brain application, centered on a stable core model, clear modular boundaries, and a lightweight runtime that turns configuration into a running system. It emphasizes separating foundational capabilities from optional extensions so services remain understandable, maintainable, and easy to evolve as the system grows. A brain is also treated as an independently deployable MCP server with identity, content, capabilities, and interfaces, supporting reusable deployment across personal, team, and community contexts while preserving clean separation of responsibilities and long-term extensibility.

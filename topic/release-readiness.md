---
title: Release Readiness
---
Release readiness is the set of criteria, checks, and preparation used to determine whether a project, product, or system is ready to ship. It focuses on confirming that work is complete enough, stable enough, and aligned with required quality, safety, scope, and operational expectations before delivery. Common readiness checks include verifying required functionality, confirming quality and reliability, resolving dependencies and blockers, reviewing risks, and ensuring packaging, onboarding, and launch support are in place. Readiness reviews and checklists provide a practical gate between development and release, helping teams avoid premature delivery, reduce risk, and make confident decisions about launch timing and scope.

---
title: Release Readiness
---
This topic addresses the criteria and preparation needed before a change can be considered ready for release. It includes the checks, expectations, and quality considerations that help ensure a contribution is safe to ship. Release readiness is framed as part of the development process, not just a final approval step. The focus suggests attention to stability, completeness, and readiness for downstream use. This makes it an important topic for teams that want predictable and reliable delivery.

---
title: Project Roadmap
---
This topic covers the structured plan that turns strategy and priorities into a sequenced view of upcoming work over time. It focuses on organizing deliverable work by value, dependency, and release timing; defining clear milestones; and balancing short-term commitments with longer-term direction. A project roadmap bridges high-level goals and concrete delivery stages, helping teams decide what should happen in upcoming release windows or milestones, make consistent tradeoffs, coordinate execution across the project lifecycle, and communicate direction and expectations to stakeholders. The emphasis is on actionable high-level planning and prioritization rather than detailed task execution.

---
title: Setup
---
Practical guidance for preparing a system, project, environment, or workspace for first use or contribution. It covers prerequisites, installation, dependency management, configuration, tool setup, workspace preparation, initialization, and initial verification needed to reach a working state. Good setup documentation is clear, ordered, concise, and reproducible so new users or contributors can get started quickly, avoid common mistakes, and reach first success with minimal friction. It is especially important in contributor-facing onboarding, where predictable setup reduces early blockers and support burden. Quickstart steps can be included when they help users reach a functional state faster.

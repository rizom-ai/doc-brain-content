---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers how to help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. It focuses on clear introductory guidance that explains the purpose or value in simple terms, sets expectations, and provides a predictable path through prerequisites, environment setup, basic orientation, first steps, and early contributions. Effective onboarding also clarifies communication norms, code review practices, and how to ask for help when relevant. Good onboarding reduces confusion and the learning curve by organizing practical instructions in an easy-to-follow way, such as checklists, setup guidance, first-task walkthroughs, and contribution rules. The goal is to reduce friction, create a positive first impression, and help newcomers become productive sooner with fewer mistakes and less drop-off.

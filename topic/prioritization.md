---
title: Prioritization
---
Prioritization is the process of deciding what should be tackled first among tasks, features, goals, or roadmap items so effort is directed to the highest-value work. It turns a broad set of possibilities into a realistic sequence by ranking options against factors such as urgency, impact, strategic fit, risk, dependencies, capacity, and readiness. In planning and delivery contexts, prioritization shapes onboarding, setup, core development, milestone scope, release order, and roadmap sequencing while balancing immediate needs with longer-term goals. Effective prioritization makes tradeoffs explicit, helps teams avoid overcommitting, reduces wasted effort on lower-value work, and keeps execution aligned with project objectives and product direction.

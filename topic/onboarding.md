---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers the process of helping new users or contributors get started quickly and confidently. It focuses on clear introductory guidance that explains the core value or concept in simple terms, outlines what to read or do first, and provides a predictable path through setup and early usage. It also includes concise onboarding documentation and first-run experiences that teach the workflow through simple, progressive steps. The goal is to reduce confusion and friction for beginners, make the product immediately understandable and approachable, and accelerate confidence and early success. A practical onboarding resource often includes a simple checklist or structured progression from first steps to effective use.

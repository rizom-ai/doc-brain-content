title: Project Roadmap
---
This topic covers the summary of roadmap priorities and the overall direction of the project. It focuses on identifying what work is planned, what changes are coming next, and how those plans shape near-term decisions. The content is meant to help people understand upcoming releases and the sequence of priorities. It also supports planning by making the current strategy easier to follow. In practice, this kind of topic is useful for aligning expectations across a team or knowledge base. It emphasizes future work rather than completed work, making it a planning-oriented concept.

---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributing to a project, documentation system, or knowledge base in a clear, consistent, and reviewable way. This topic covers contributor expectations, standards, etiquette, and practical steps for proposing, preparing, submitting, reviewing, and integrating changes safely and predictably. It includes norms, conventions, and repeatable workflows that help new contributors understand what good contributions look like and align their work with project expectations from the start. Strong contribution guidance should be easy to follow, reduce friction, preserve clean structure, avoid duplication, reuse existing patterns, and support navigation, future updates, and coordination between contributors and maintainers. The goal is to make participation explicit, improve quality, surface structural issues early, minimize confusion, and scale collaboration through a shared process.

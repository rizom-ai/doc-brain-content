---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Standards and habits for making effective contributions to a shared project, codebase, or documentation set. This topic covers the expectations contributors should follow in a repository or team environment, including how to prepare work, follow project conventions, structure changes, and submit contributions in a way that is easy to review and maintain. It includes guidance on branch usage, commit quality, pull request or review structure, communication of progress, quality checks, and preparing work for merge. The focus is on making implicit workflow and decision-making conventions explicit, reducing friction for both internal and external contributors, improving safety and quality for maintainers, and supporting repeatable collaboration that avoids confusion or inconsistency.

---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers guidance and documentation that help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. It focuses on reducing friction for first-time setup and orientation by providing a clear overview, essential concepts, prerequisites, introductory steps, and the information needed to begin using or contributing effectively. Good onboarding shortens time to productivity by making expectations, workflows, and next actions easy to understand, often through setup instructions, checklists, orientation pages, contribution guides, and first-task walkthroughs. It may also explain what to install, how to run the project locally, communication norms, review or contribution practices, and where to ask for help, lowering the barrier to entry and supporting smooth adoption.

---
title: Brain Architecture
---
The structural design of a brain application or platform, centered on a stable core model with clear modular boundaries, so the system remains understandable, maintainable, and easy to evolve. It separates core services from surrounding capabilities to reduce coupling, isolate change, and make system behavior easier to reason about. A strong brain architecture supports optional extensions through plugins or defined extension points while keeping the foundation stable. It also treats the brain as a reusable platform or independently deployable service with clear identity, content, capabilities, and interfaces, suitable for personal, team, and community use over time.

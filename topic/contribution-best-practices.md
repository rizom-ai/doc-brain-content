---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributing effectively to a project through clear, consistent, and reviewable workflows. This topic covers contributor expectations, standards, etiquette, and practical steps for proposing, preparing, and submitting changes, including code, documentation, and other content contributions. It emphasizes shared conventions for how to add, edit, and maintain content in alignment with existing project structure rather than making ad hoc edits. Good contribution guidance reduces ambiguity, improves collaboration between new and experienced contributors, and makes submission and review processes smoother, more predictable, and easier to maintain over time.

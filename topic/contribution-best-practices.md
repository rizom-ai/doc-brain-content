---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributing to a project, documentation system, or knowledge base in a clear, consistent, and reviewable way. This topic covers contributor expectations, standards, etiquette, and the practical workflow for proposing, preparing, submitting, reviewing, and integrating changes safely and predictably. It focuses on helping contributors align with project goals and maintainers’ expectations, improve the quality and consistency of their work, and reduce review overhead through clear norms set in advance. Strong contribution guidance reduces ambiguity and friction, improves communication and review, avoids duplication, preserves clean structure, supports navigation and future updates, and helps scale collaboration through shared practices that produce predictable, high-quality changes.

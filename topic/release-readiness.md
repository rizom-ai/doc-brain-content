---
title: Release Readiness
---
Release readiness is the criteria, checks, and preparation used to determine whether a project, product, or content is ready to ship. It focuses on confirming what is complete, verifying that priorities and delivery expectations are aligned, and identifying what is still missing before launch. Typical practices include readiness checklists, milestone reviews, and final validation to confirm completeness, quality, consistency, packaging, dependencies, and alignment with release scope. Release readiness functions as a practical quality gate between development and launch, helping teams reduce uncertainty, prevent shipping unfinished or misaligned work, and make confident, predictable release decisions.

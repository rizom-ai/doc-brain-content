---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Standards and habits for making effective contributions to a shared project, team process, or documentation set. This topic covers the expectations contributors should follow in a repository or team environment, including how to document team norms so contributions remain consistent, maintainable, and aligned with project goals. It includes guidance on structuring work, submitting changes, communicating progress, applying quality checks, and preparing work for review and merge. The focus is on making implicit workflow and decision-making conventions explicit, reducing friction for new and existing contributors, improving safety and quality for maintainers, and supporting repeatable collaboration that avoids confusion or inconsistency.

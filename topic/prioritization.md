---
title: Prioritization
---
This topic is about ranking roadmap items based on importance, impact, and fit with product goals. It includes deciding which tasks deserve attention first and how to convert a list of possible work into a clear priority order. The content emphasizes that priorities should be derived from product direction, not just feature requests or loose ideas. Prioritization is essential for keeping development focused and preventing roadmap sprawl. In a brain platform context, it likely supports deciding which capabilities most improve the system's usefulness or extensibility. This topic matters because it is the mechanism that turns planning into a manageable execution plan.

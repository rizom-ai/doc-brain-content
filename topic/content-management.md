---
title: Content Management
---
Content management is the ongoing process of creating, organizing, reviewing, updating, and maintaining documentation or other written knowledge so it remains accurate, clear, and useful over time. It includes structuring information for easy navigation and comprehension, using consistent naming and language, and arranging material so it can be found, understood, and reused efficiently. In collaborative environments, content management also involves keeping documentation aligned with current functionality or project state, applying consistent grouping and linking conventions, and supporting review, synchronization, and publishing workflows that keep the knowledge base maintainable as it grows.

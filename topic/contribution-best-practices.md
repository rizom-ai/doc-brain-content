---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributing effectively to a project through clear, consistent, and reviewable workflows. This topic covers contributor expectations, standards, etiquette, and practical steps for proposing, preparing, and submitting changes, including development work, documentation updates, and other content contributions. It emphasizes writing and following contribution guidelines that explain how people should add, edit, or maintain content, aligning with existing project structure and conventions, and creating shared conventions instead of ad hoc edits. Clear best practices reduce errors, minimize friction during review, support collaboration with both new and experienced contributors, and help sustain high-quality contributions over time.

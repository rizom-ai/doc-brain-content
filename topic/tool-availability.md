---
title: Tool Availability
---
A substantial part of the exchange is about what tools are available in the interface. The assistant lists content tools for creating, reading, updating, deleting, searching, listing, and extracting content, along with broader categories such as sync, conversation, analytics, site, and agent tools. It also reports which daemons are running, including production, preview, MCP, Discord, A2A, and webserver services. This topic captures the operational capability of the environment and the boundaries of what actions can be performed from the current interface.

AI Grant is a grant program for open source projects, offering $5,000 to $50,000 in support with no strings attached. The page notes that grants may be provided as compute or cash, and it links to a separate accelerator program at aigrant.com.

The page highlights recent grantees such as abetlen, philpax, and TySam, and then lists many prior grants to researchers and builders working on a wide range of AI-related projects, from reinforcement learning and model management to drug design, medical imaging, generative art, and privacy tools. It closes by noting that AI Grant was established in 2017 by Nat Friedman and Daniel Gross.

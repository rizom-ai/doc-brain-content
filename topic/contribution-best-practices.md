---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributing changes to a project in a clear, consistent, and reviewable way. This topic covers contributor expectations, standards, etiquette, and practical steps for proposing, preparing, submitting, and applying changes safely and predictably. It includes conventions for code, documentation, and other content contributions, along with workflow practices such as review, branching, local development, and coordination among multiple contributors. The goal is to align contributors with project norms from the start, reduce ambiguity and review overhead, improve collaboration, and make contribution processes easier to follow and maintain over time.

---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers the process of helping new users or contributors get started quickly and confidently. It focuses on clear introductory guidance that explains the core concept in simple terms, outlines what to read or do first, and provides a predictable path through setup and early usage. The goal is to reduce confusion and friction for beginners by making the initial experience easy, understandable, and reusable across similar situations. A practical onboarding resource often includes a simple checklist or structured progression from first steps to effective use.

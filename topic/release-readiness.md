---
title: Release Readiness
---
Release readiness is the set of criteria, checks, and preparation steps used to determine whether software, modules, or content are ready to ship. It ensures work is complete enough, reviewed, validated, packaged, and aligned with expectations before launch. Typical practices include readiness checklists, milestone reviews, and final validation to confirm completeness, accuracy, consistency, formatting or packaging, and fit with the current release scope. Release readiness is an ongoing quality gate throughout the workflow rather than a one-time approval, helping teams coordinate version planning, reduce missed steps, and make safe, predictable release decisions.

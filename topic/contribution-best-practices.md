---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Recommended standards and habits for making effective contributions to a shared project, team process, or documentation set. This topic covers how to define clear expectations for proposing, reviewing, and merging changes so contributions stay consistent over time. It includes guidance on structuring work, communicating progress, and preparing changes for review, while keeping content clear, maintainable, and aligned with existing patterns. The focus is on reducing friction for new contributors, improving safety and quality for maintainers, and creating a repeatable approach to collaboration that avoids confusion or inconsistency.

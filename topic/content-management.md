---
title: Content Management
---
Content management is the organization, maintenance, and lifecycle handling of markdown-based knowledge content so it remains easy to navigate, update, search, and publish. In this system, durable knowledge is stored as typed entities with YAML frontmatter and markdown bodies, and those entities can be created, updated, searched, extracted, and synced through shared tools and workflows. The topic includes structuring information for clear discovery and onboarding, keeping content current and consistent, managing publishing statuses and generation workflows, handling image references and prompt customization, and maintaining the entity graph that connects notes, links, posts, decks, newsletters, topics, and summaries. Strong content management keeps the knowledge base reliable, scalable, and useful as a source of truth for both readers and agents.

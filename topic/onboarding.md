---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers guidance and documentation that help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. It focuses on reducing first-time friction by clearly explaining prerequisites, setup steps, the basic workflow, and essential concepts in an accessible way. Strong onboarding shortens the learning curve and time to productivity by making expectations, common early issues, next steps, and sources of help easy to find. It often includes setup instructions, introductory overviews, checklists, contribution guidance, first-task walkthroughs, and pointers to where to ask for help. Good onboarding supports initial adoption and contributor confidence while also making a project easier to extend by creating a clear, welcoming starting point for newcomers.

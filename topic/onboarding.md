---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers guidance and documentation that help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. It focuses on reducing first-time friction by providing clear prerequisites, setup steps, quickstarts, essential concepts, contribution guidance, and first-use explanations in an accessible way. Effective onboarding shortens the learning curve and time to value by guiding people from their first interaction through the core steps they need to understand the system, while making expectations, common issues, next steps, and sources of help easy to find. It often includes orientation material, checklists, walkthroughs, examples, and pointers to support. Good onboarding improves adoption, participation, and engagement while creating a welcoming, low-barrier starting point for newcomers.

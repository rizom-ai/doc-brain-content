---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributing to a project in a clear, consistent, and reviewable way. This topic covers contributor expectations, standards, etiquette, and practical steps for proposing, preparing, submitting, and applying changes safely and predictably. It includes conventions for code, documentation, and other content contributions, along with workflow practices such as review, branching, local development, and coordination between contributors and maintainers. The focus is on making contribution instructions explicit so people know how to participate effectively and align with project norms, reducing ambiguity, improving quality, and minimizing coordination overhead.

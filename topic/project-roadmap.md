---
title: Project Roadmap
---
This topic covers the structured plan that translates strategy and product goals into a sequenced view of upcoming work. It focuses on deciding what belongs in future planning, how priorities are organized over time, and how milestones, releases, scope, and dependencies shape the order of execution. A project roadmap serves as the bridge between high-level intent and development direction, helping teams make consistent tradeoffs, align on what matters most, and communicate expectations to stakeholders. The emphasis is on high-level planning and prioritization rather than detailed task execution, making it useful for understanding what is coming next and why.

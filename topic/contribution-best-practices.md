---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributors on how to extend a project or shared system in ways that are consistent, reviewable, and easy to maintain. This topic covers clear contribution instructions, expected workflows, and the standards contributors should follow when preparing changes, aligning with existing structure, and communicating progress. It is especially useful for onboarding new contributors such as plugin authors or developers, while still supporting experienced maintainers with a repeatable process. Strong contribution practices reduce friction, improve consistency, preserve quality, and make it easier for a project to grow through outside participation.

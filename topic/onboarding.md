---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers how to help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. It focuses on clear introductory guidance that explains the purpose or value in simple terms, sets expectations, and provides a predictable path through environment setup, basic orientation, first steps, and early contributions. Good onboarding reduces confusion and the learning curve by organizing practical instructions in an easy-to-follow way, such as checklists, setup guidance, first-task walkthroughs, and contribution rules. The goal is to reduce friction, create a positive first impression, and help newcomers become productive sooner with fewer mistakes and less drop-off.

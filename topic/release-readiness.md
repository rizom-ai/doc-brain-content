---
title: Release Readiness
---
Release readiness is the set of criteria, checks, and preparation used to determine whether a project, product, or system is ready to ship. It focuses on confirming that the work is complete enough, stable enough, and aligned with required quality, safety, and scope expectations before delivery. Common readiness checks include verifying required functionality, resolving dependencies and blockers, reviewing risks, and validating that packaging, onboarding, and operational requirements are in place. Readiness reviews and checklists act as a practical gate between development and launch, helping teams avoid premature release, reduce risk, and make confident, predictable decisions about release timing and scope.

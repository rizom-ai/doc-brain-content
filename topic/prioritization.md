---
title: Prioritization
---
Prioritization is the process of deciding which tasks or roadmap items matter most so development effort goes where it will have the greatest value. It involves ranking work by urgency, impact, strategic fit, and dependencies, then turning a broad set of possible tasks into a clear order of execution. Good prioritization keeps planning aligned with product direction, prevents roadmap sprawl, and reduces wasted effort on lower-value work while important items remain outstanding. It is a foundational mechanism for focused decision-making, release planning, and managing development work effectively.

---
title: Setup
---
This topic covers the initial installation, preparation, and configuration needed to make a project, environment, or workspace ready for first use or contribution. It typically includes prerequisites, dependency installation, environment-specific requirements, first-run configuration, and practical setup checklists that help users get started predictably. Good setup documentation is explicit enough for newcomers to follow without extra support, reducing ambiguity, troubleshooting, and early drop-off. It is a foundational topic for documentation aimed at developers, contributors, or users who need to prepare a local or new environment before deeper work begins.

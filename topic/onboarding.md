---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers guidance and documentation that help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. It focuses on reducing friction for first-time setup and orientation by explaining the system, workflow, and essential concepts in an accessible way. Strong onboarding shortens the learning curve and time to productivity by making expectations, prerequisites, and next steps easy to understand. It often includes setup instructions, introductory overviews, checklists, contribution guidance, first-task walkthroughs, and pointers to where to ask for help. Good onboarding not only supports initial adoption, but also makes a project easier to extend by creating a clear, welcoming starting point for newcomers.

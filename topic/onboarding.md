---
title: Onboarding
---
Guidance and documentation that help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. Onboarding reduces first-time friction by making the entry experience clear, efficient, and welcoming. It typically includes prerequisites, setup steps, quickstarts, first-run flows, checklists, essential concepts, contribution guidance, examples, walkthroughs, and pointers to help. Good onboarding structures information so newcomers can easily find what they need, understand expectations and project norms, avoid common issues, and take the next steps toward productive use or contribution. Effective onboarding shortens the learning curve, improves adoption and retention, reduces support burden, and helps people become productive sooner.

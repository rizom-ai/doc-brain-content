---
title: Project Roadmap
---
A project roadmap is a high-level, time-based plan that organizes strategy, priorities, and ideas into a sequenced path of work. It shows how work will progress over a defined horizon by grouping major workstreams, milestones, deliverables, dependencies, and release timing into phased stages. The roadmap helps teams decide what happens next, what can wait, and how to balance competing priorities when time or resources are limited.

Unlike detailed task management, a roadmap focuses on planning, prioritization, and delivery sequencing at a broader level. It connects long-term direction with near-term execution, supports tradeoff decisions, and makes the intended progression of work clear to stakeholders. A strong roadmap is both structured and adaptable, providing enough guidance to coordinate work and communicate expectations while remaining flexible as goals, constraints, and opportunities change.

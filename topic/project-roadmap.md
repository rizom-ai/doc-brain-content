---
title: Project Roadmap
---
This topic covers the structured plan that turns strategy and project goals into a sequenced view of upcoming work over time. It focuses on defining short-, medium-, and longer-term milestones; organizing priorities by value, dependency, and release timing; and deciding what belongs in the next phase versus what can wait. A project roadmap serves as a bridge between high-level intent and execution direction, helping teams make consistent tradeoffs, coordinate development across the project lifecycle, and communicate expectations to stakeholders. The emphasis is on high-level planning and prioritization rather than detailed task execution, making it useful for understanding what is coming next and why.

---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributors on how to extend a project or shared system in ways that are consistent, reviewable, and easy to maintain. This topic covers the expectations, norms, and workflows contributors should follow when preparing changes, aligning with existing structure, and communicating progress. It is especially useful for onboarding new contributors such as plugin authors or developers, while also supporting experienced maintainers with a repeatable process. Clear contribution guidance reduces friction, improves consistency, preserves quality, and helps projects grow through outside participation with long-term maintainability in mind.

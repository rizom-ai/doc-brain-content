title: Extensibility
---
This topic describes the broader design principle of building a system that can evolve through well-defined extension mechanisms. In this case, extensibility is presented as a central objective of the brain application’s architecture, not just a secondary feature. The focus is on designing boundaries and interfaces that let new capabilities be added cleanly. It also implies attention to maintainability, since extensible systems need stable core abstractions to avoid tight coupling. The skill likely encourages thinking about how future contributors can add features safely and predictably. This makes extensibility a reusable umbrella topic that captures the overall design intent behind the architecture and plugin model.

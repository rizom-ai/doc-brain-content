---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers the process of helping new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently. It focuses on clear introductory guidance that explains the core value or purpose in simple terms, sets expectations, and provides a predictable path through setup, early usage, and first contributions. Good onboarding reduces confusion and the learning curve by offering practical instructions early, such as checklists, structured first steps, or contribution guidelines that make participation easier. The goal is to reduce friction, shape positive early impressions, and help beginners become productive sooner with fewer mistakes and less drop-off.

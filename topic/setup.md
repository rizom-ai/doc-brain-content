---
title: Setup
---
Practical guidance for preparing a system, project, environment, or workspace for first use or contribution. It covers prerequisites, installation, dependency management, configuration, access, tool setup, workspace preparation, initialization, and initial verification needed to reach a working state. Good setup documentation is clear, ordered, concise, and reproducible so first-time users and contributors can get started quickly, avoid common mistakes, and reach first success with minimal friction. It should be direct and actionable, easy to scan, and present the required tools and sequence of steps in a practical order, often using a checklist when helpful. Quickstart steps can be included when they help users reach a functional state faster, and setup guidance is especially valuable during launch preparation because it improves usability, repeatability, and reduces support needs.

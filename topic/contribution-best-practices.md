---
title: Contribution Best Practices
---
Guidance for contributing changes to a project in a clear, consistent, and reviewable way. This topic covers contributor expectations, standards, etiquette, and practical steps for proposing, preparing, submitting, and applying changes safely and predictably. It includes conventions for code, documentation, and other content contributions, along with workflow practices such as review, branching, local development, coordination among multiple contributors, and writing contribution instructions that clarify what to do, how to do it, and what standards to follow. The goal is to reduce ambiguity, improve collaboration, reduce errors, and make contribution and review processes easier to follow and maintain over time.

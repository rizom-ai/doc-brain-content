---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers guidance and documentation that help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. It focuses on reducing first-time friction by providing clear prerequisites, setup steps, quickstarts, basic workflows, and essential concepts in an accessible way. Effective onboarding shortens the learning curve and time to value by making expectations, early tasks, common issues, next steps, and sources of help easy to find. It often includes orientation material, checklists, first-task walkthroughs, contribution guidance, and pointers to support. Good onboarding improves adoption and engagement while creating a welcoming, low-barrier starting point for newcomers.

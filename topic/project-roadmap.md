---
title: Project Roadmap
---
A project roadmap is a structured, time-based plan that translates strategy and priorities into a sequenced view of upcoming work. It organizes deliverables by value, dependency, milestone, and release timing over a defined horizon such as a quarter or several months. A roadmap helps teams turn backlog items and scattered tasks into a coherent timeline, balancing ambition with feasibility while maintaining alignment to broader goals.

The topic emphasizes high-level planning and prioritization rather than detailed task execution. It supports tradeoff decisions, coordination across the project or product lifecycle, and clear communication to stakeholders about scope, direction, expectations, and what will be delivered when. A strong roadmap leaves room to adapt as priorities change while still providing enough structure to guide near-term commitments and longer-term direction.

---
title: Prioritization
---
Prioritization is the process of evaluating and ranking tasks, goals, or initiatives so limited time and capacity go to the highest-value work first. It turns an open-ended backlog into an actionable sequence by weighing criteria such as importance, urgency, impact, strategic fit, feasibility, risk, dependencies, readiness, and available resources. In product and project planning, it informs roadmap structure, backlog grooming, milestone sequencing, and release scope by clarifying what should happen next and what can wait. Effective prioritization helps teams make tradeoffs explicit, reduce overload and decision fatigue, avoid overcommitment, and stay aligned with goals and constraints.

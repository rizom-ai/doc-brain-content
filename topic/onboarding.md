---
title: Onboarding
---
This topic covers documentation and guidance that help new users, contributors, or team members get started quickly and confidently with a project, product, or process. It includes a clear project or product overview, the basic concepts needed to understand the system, setup and prerequisites, first steps, and an easy path toward initial tasks or contributions. Effective onboarding also sets expectations by explaining communication norms, contribution or review practices, and where to ask for help when relevant. Good onboarding reduces friction and the learning curve by organizing practical instructions in a predictable, approachable way, such as checklists, setup guides, orientation pages, and first-task walkthroughs. The goal is to move someone from first exposure to productive participation with minimal confusion, mistakes, or drop-off.

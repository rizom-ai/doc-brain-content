---
title: Brain Architecture
---
The structural design of a brain application or platform, centered on a stable core model with clear modular boundaries. It defines the main services, their responsibilities, and how they fit together so the system remains understandable, maintainable, and easy to evolve as it grows. A strong brain architecture separates core functionality from surrounding capabilities to reduce coupling, isolate change, and make behavior easier to reason about. It also supports extensibility through plugins or defined extension points while preserving a cohesive core, making the system easier to adapt, maintain, and scale over time.

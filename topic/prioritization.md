---
title: Prioritization
---
Prioritization is the process of deciding which tasks, features, or roadmap items should be done first so effort is directed toward the highest-value work. It involves ranking work by factors such as urgency, impact, strategic fit, risk, and dependencies, then translating a broad set of possibilities into a clear sequence of execution. Effective prioritization balances immediate needs with longer-term goals, supports planning capacity, and ensures important dependencies are handled in the right order. It helps prevent scattered effort, reduces waste on lower-value work, and keeps development aligned with product direction, release planning, and overall project goals.
